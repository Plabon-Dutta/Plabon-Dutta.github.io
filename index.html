About Me

I am Jayonto Dutta Plabon, a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Asia Pacific (UAP), Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I am also completing my Master of Science in Computer Science (expected July 2026). My academic and professional journey is driven by a deep passion for research and a strong aspiration to pursue doctoral studies.

My primary research interests span Large Language Models (LLMs), Federated Learning (FL), Explainable AI (X-AI), Generative AI, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and IoT. I have published research in high-impact, peer-reviewed venues including Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence, IEEE Access, and Computers and Electrical Engineering, as well as contributing book chapters to Elsevier publications.

Alongside my academic responsibilities, I serve as Co-convenor of the UAP Programming Contest Club, mentoring students in competitive programming. As an ICPC Regionalist (2021, 2023, 2024) and Codeforces Specialist, I bring rigorous analytical thinking into my research practice.

I am currently seeking PhD opportunities where I can deepen my expertise, contribute meaningfully to the global research community, and work alongside leading scholars to address real-world challenges through intelligent systems.
